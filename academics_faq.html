  • question_answerQ.1 What all Centres presently exist at IIT Jodhpur?

    The institute emphasises multidisciplinary education in its undergraduate curriculum and interdisciplinary specialisations at the postgraduate level. At present IIT Jodhpur has four Centres :
    Energy
    Information and Computer Technology
    System Sciences
    Biologically Inspired System Science.

  • question_answerQ.2 What academic programmes are being offered?

    IIT Jodhpur has a different perspective towards academics with established Centres. At present IIT Jodhpur provides the following programmes:
    B.Tech in the following disciplines: Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, System Science and Biologically inspired system science (BISS, which is being introduced from academic year 2013).
    M.Tech programmes are offered through COE in 1) Energy, 2)Information and Computer Technology (ICT) and 3)System Sciences
    Ph.D programmes offered through COE in 1) Energy, 2) Information and Computer Technology (ICT), 3) System Sciences and 4) Biologically inspired system sciences.

  • question_answerQ.3 How are the classroom facilities?

    IIT Jodhpur has well equipped AC classrooms with projector facility in each classroom.

  • question_answerQ.4 Is there a Central Library in the campus?

    Yes, the Central Library of IIT Jodhpur supports and facilitates the teaching, learning and research activities of the Institute. The library presently has around 5000 books and also subscribes to various magazines. Access to E-journals is also available.
    Library IIT Jodhpur

  • question_answerQ.6 Is there a Computer Centre for the students?

    The Institute has a modern Computer Centre presently running on a gigabit LAN with 1Gbps internet bandwidth. The computer centre is functional both at institute and residential campus.
    Computer Centre IIT Jodhpur

  • question_answerQ.7 What other facilities are available near the institute?

    You can find everything near the institute. Post office, banks, ATMs, general stores, restaurants, theatres etc., all are within 1 kilometre away from the Institute.
