Nurturing Understanding Technology and Science (N.U.T.S)

The science and technology council of IITJ provides you an opportunity to explore the amazing world of science and technology… to innovate, to create, learn and experience with lots of fun! We provide you a platform to show your passion towards technology and to think beyond the conventional boundaries of science. Here we make things fly, reveal the working of complicated devices, make our own robots and witness the beauty of stars.

Clubs Under N.U.T.S

1) Aero-Modelling Club

Here in this club you learn a great deal about basics of flying machines to making one yourself. While having fun you will also be surprised and fascinated to witness your own models fly! Water rockets, gliders, RC planes, hovercrafts, parachutes everything. If there is a thing that can fly, then we teach you how to build it and make it fly. Competitions are held throughout the year to give students a competitive edge and enhance their creativity and knowledge. The club has been actively involved in lot of projects, recently built a light-weight RC plane and Hovercraft, UAV and Quad-copter. Apart from hostel activities, club participates in many national competitions, this year a team from aero modelling club has been selected to compete with best young minds in the country in DRDO MICAV competition. The coming year, the club intends to work on solar powered planes, ornithopters, multi-staged water rockets, UAV’s and many more projects to be undertaken.

 
2) Automobile Club

This club is all about vehicles, everything from a simple cycle to F1 car, with NOX burning out of them is taught under this club. May it be the engine, suspension, hydraulics or may it be the whole body of the vehicle, you will design it with your own hands. The automobile club has associations with worldwide established automobile communities like SAE (Society of Automobile Engineers) and has hands-on experience in nationwide competitions like BAJA - an all-terrain racer. The eco-friendly vehicle designed by the club in the internationally held event, EFFICYCLE received a huge applause from the judges along with the community of the EFFICYCLE. Very first car of this club inaugurated by MHRD minister Dr. M. M. Pallam Raju was one of the major events of this year. And now, our budding engineers are designing a green motor vehicle, which will not only run on green fuel but would also be economical and convenient.

 
3) Astronomy Club

Enchanted by the beauties and the mysteries of the cosmos? Do you feel the shiver when you look up at the stars in the night?At Astronomy Club, IIT Jodhpur, all your curiosities will find answers. From topics like Black Holes, Relativity, Space-Time and alien existence to real observation sessions where you can actually view the beauty of the universe. We currently have a 6” Newtonian telescope, which is powerful enough to resolve star clusters, planets and even distant galaxies! Add to it seminars, webinars and sessions, including the one we had this IGNUS by Dr. Adam Guy Reiss, Nobel Laureate and celebrated astrophysicist. Don’t worry if you don’t know much, all we ask for is passion and a burning quest for the unknown and the unanswered! 26

 
4) Electronics Club

If this digitalized world is your passion then Electronics Club is the right place for you. This is a place where you will have hands on experience on breadboards, PCB’s and micro controllers. Yearlong activities of electronics club include lectures, workshops and many more competitions. Rather than just being a club, it is a place where students sit together, research and discuss latest technologies and then come up with entirely something new of their own. The GSM circuit of your mobile, the GPS application in it, microphones, technologies like radio frequencies, wireless instant communication, all of which till today you just have used, tomorrow you will be making them on your own. Ping Pong, solar tracking system, PCB designing, flasher circuits, line follower, digital clock and many more of such projects were successfully done by E-club. And the club has planned to do some exciting projects like wireless communication, controlling devices with voice acknowledgement and many more.

 
5) Robotics Club

Robotics – heart of science and technology around the world. And robotics club IITJ is no more less to this theme. Yearlong activities of robotics club include lectures and workshops on nearly all the recent technologies in the field of robotics. In this club you are going to get the chance to make not just any, but every robot you may have some day dreamt of. May it be, football playing, or a load picking, maze solving or an image processing, motion sensing or an ultimate humanoid, every form of modern robotics is taught and researched over in our robotics club.   Club has worked on technologies like DTMF, wireless communication, algorithms based on localization and mappings (SLAM) and many more. Teams from this club participate in many of the national competitions, different tech festivals and many more.Club has planned to work on implementation of various algorithms on robots, rubik’s cube solver robot and all terrain robot powered by solar energy. Robo-Hockey and Robowars were the eye catching events organized by robotics club in IGNUS – inter college technocultural festival IGNUS

 
6) Programming Club

The coolest club of IIT Jodhpur, a place for the wannabe geeks – the Programming Club. This club will sail you through the world of algorithms and programming with the latest technologies. With a variety of projects specializing from the desktop, web and mobile platform we have it all. We have events from writing crypted codes, to the AI challenges to create your own virtual player. We have organized events in the past in association with Mozilla Foundation and participated in the national finals of ACM ICPC. From having the latest server infrastructure to our own online programming contest platforms to having open-source projects we have it all.

 
7) Web Designing Club

In this modern world every organization, company or institution requires its own website to provide information about it. Web-Designing club of IITJ plays an important role in providing students the basic knowledge to build a website. Regular lectures, workshops and competitions makes web designing club a cool yet creative and innovative source of working. Apart from these activities the club has designed websites for the various institute festivals. Further some online competitions were also organized by the club. Designing an online portal for IITJ under SCHoD which have features like an effective Grievance redressal System, detailed Minute of the Meetings, and notice Board with the latest happenings is the running project.

 

Media, Arts and Design (M.A.D)

We are M.A.D., the most creative and productive council of IIT Jodhpur, born out of the vision of “blending the technology with innovation and design” along with the entertainment and creativity. Besides running its own activities, workshops and competitions etc, the council plays important role in assisting all the other council and institute activities as well. Be it media screening, designing logos, creating posters, capturing moments, editing films, animating objects etc., we’ve got it all covered. The council consists of various clubs run by official coordinators, assistant coordinators, mentors and club members, all under the direct supervision of the General Executive and the Council Mentor

Clubs Under M.A.D

1) Designerds

Designing Club is the base for every other club in M.A.D. The club consists of three teams of “GRAPHICS DESIGNING”, “JOUR MAGAZINE” and “WEB DESIGNING”, which create artistic posters and brochures, publish newsletters and semester-magazine, give presentable themes to a website connecting people. Infusing these skill amongst students through competitive workshops and taking long-time projects from time to time, club has been able to create some professional designers in the students. Engineers with the knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator and In-design are ultimately E++. The club also helps the students to efficiently make their thoughts presentable on paper.

 
2) Photography and Photo-editing Club

“All photographs are memento mori. To take a photograph is to participate in another person’s (or thing’s) mortality, vulnerability, mutability. Precisely by slicing out this moment and freezing it, all photographs testify to time’s relentless melt.” It’s one the most hardworking, energetic and enjoyable club. Its lively throughout the year, the most active during the fest. The club consistently hosts its workshops and competitions on Photography Skills and various types of Digital Arts. The coming year, we aim in culminating photography as an art using technologies such as DSLR, editing via different software, visiting historic places etc . Join the club and cultivate your desire for photography.

 
3) FRAME-X (The Film making and Video-editing Club)

A ‘Movie’ is born first in some head, dies on paper; is resuscitated by the living persons and real objects used which are killed on film but, placed in a certain order and projected on to a screen, come to life again like flowers in water. As an integral part of technological arts, FRAME-X is the combination of “VIDEO EDITING”, “FILM MAKING” and “MEDIA SCREENING” teams. The club always focuses on bringing innovation, creativity, productivity and directivity in the videos we make, record, edit and screen. Using the most sophisticated Video-recording Cameras and the softwares like Windows Movie Maker, Sony Vegas and Adobe After-Effects, the club members record, create and edit short films and numerous videos including the trailers of IGNUS and VARCHAS.

 
4) Animation Club

The Animation club tries to bring out the real animator in you. The club organizes various workshops at regular interval by its own members and professionals. As a blend of “Stop-motion Animators” and “Software-motion Animators”, the club has been introducing new concepts in animation like pixilation, claymation, paper-animation, 2-D & 3-D graphics animation, and flash-light animation etc. The club collaborates with The Animation Garage, one of the renowned animation studio in India and organizes various workshops with it on these concepts. Animation Club of IIT Jodhpur makes various animated videos for IGNUS and our very own MAD festival “Kalakshep” and organises various events during the academic year and in the fests giving a platform to all the students to show up their talents.

 
5) ATELIERS (The Fine Arts Club)

‘ATELIER’ is the French word for “workshop”, and in English is used principally for the workshop of an artist in the fine or decorative arts. Vision of the club is to provide a multitude of opportunities to all the students of IIT Jodhpur by offering them new criteria for aesthetical thoughts and training them in the various cultural and modern art, that enables them to develop and show their creative talents, creative vision, critical thinking and cooperative learning. From Fresher’s Night to Institute level festivals and competitions, Fine arts club is involved in presenting the actual themes to the scene. Apart from these, the club organises gobs of activities including painting and sketching, glass etching, inter-block events, face and t-shirt painting, tattoo designing, clay modeling, wax carving, graffiti workshops etc.

 

Public Relations Organization Management (P.R.O.M.)

PROM is one among the six councils of SCHoD, IIT Jodhpur. As the name suggests, it deals with all matters regarding public relations, and management of the various activities that take place in the institute. It helps in enhancing one’s personality, management skills, public speaking, writing and coordination with colleagues, which leads to an overall development in him/her.

 
Leadership Enhancement and All-round Development (LEAD)

This is a new initiative by PROM, which aims at enhancing the overall personality and soft skills of the students, and prepares them for the professional world. Workshops organized by professionals skilled in personality grooming, resume writing, interview etc are conducted. Group discussions, mock interviews and public-speaking sessions are conducted to train students for interviews and help in their personality development. Students get a real-world exposure, while getting expert guidance not only from professionals, but also senior mentors.

 
PROM looks after the following four sections, as the name suggests :

Promotion
Relation
Occasion
Management

The following are the cells under PROM :

1) Public Relations Cell

Public Relation is very important for a social life. Its main motive is to make students more interactive while developing new relations with the students of various institutes throughout the country. Through this you get a chance to express yourself better, learn more, make new friends which will make you more comfortable while talking to new people, and add a flavour to your social life

 
2) Marketing cell

Finance is the key word for this cell. The main objective of this cell is to get sponsors for various inter and intra college festivals. You get to learn the way of communication with higher officials, and understanding business ethics.

 
3) Public Speaking and Personality Development Cell

Your personality is what reflects, and public speaking is one very important aspect which shows your personality. If you want to hone your public speaking skills, increase your confidence levels and add that little bit of zing to your personality, then this is the place to be!

 
4) Content Cell

The content cell caters mainly to the inter collegiate magazine of IIT Jodhpur. It also coordinates the content for the media to be published. If you have a literary sensibility, if words are a treat and you enjoy writing then content team is where you should be.

 
5) Entrepreneurship cell

All those who want to be budding entrepreneurs at some point of time in their lives will cherish being a member of this cell. This cell collaborates with the E-cells of other institutes and conducts various seminars and useful activities related to entrepreneurship. One initiative of this cell- the IITJ Conclave is a much awaited event.

 
6) Resource Management cellb

The main work of this cell is to manage resources during various inter and intra-level activities efficiently. This cell is for people who have a flair for management and are capable of handling on the spot deficits.

2) Drama Club

The dramatics club performs a variety of plays including social satires and street plays which are both didactic and simultaneously provide wholesome entertainment. Nukkad performance is the highlight of the Republic and Independence day celebration in the institute.

 
3) Music Club

Are you one of those people with a tune in your head all the time? Is music your life force? Or are you a mere bathroom singer? Do not worry! Our music club is created to cater to all your needs. Here you can learn to play an instrument, catch an uninterrupted beat and just simply unwind. The music room is well equipped with the latest instruments for those who want to come and immerse in this SANGAM!

 
4) Literature Club

Do you see yourself as a budding poet or a potential novelist? Do you have a forte for writing? If this fits your bill, then the literature club is the place to be. Come and explore a whole new world of books, belles-lettres and the magic of words. Do you have that incessant inquisitiveness and that flair for quizzing? If you enjoy the idea of sparring mentally, and love a good quiz, come be a part! It is not necessary to be a regular or experienced quizzer, its just the flair that counts. Not only will you get proper grooming and regular practice, but great chances of exposure at several national and intra-collegiate quizzes.

 

Sports, Adventures, Games and Explorations (S.A.G.E.)

The Sports, Adventure, Games and Explorations council aims to promote sportsmanship and team work. Stupendous performances by the IIT Jodhpur team at inter IIT sports meets; inter-collegiate (Varchas) and intra-collegiate sports fests testify the success of the council. IIT Jodhpur team bagged 1 gold, 5 silver and 6 bronze medals in Inter-IIT sports meets. We won the overall champions trophy in Varchas’12. In addition to sports, the club arranges several adventure tours and coordinates the carom club, the chess club, the skating club and yoga club. This year the sports council plans to introduce new clubs like jogger’s club, karate club and even proposes to organise an archery camp.

Our Mission

1.To motivate students to work with team spirit.
2.To promote sports and exercise in the true spirit of sportsmanship.
3.To create programs that facilitates athletes to perform well.
4.To improve institute performance at the Inter- IIT sports meet every year.
5.To inculcate values of perseverance, tenacity and a never say die attitude.
6.To create a sense of confidence and to nurture leadership abilities.

Council Activities

The Sports Council has been an active part of the student body since the beginning. The institute takes prides in organizing its very own inter college national level sports fest in the name of “VARCHAS”. The success of Varchas has largely been due to the student’s exceptional organizational and leadership abilities.
The sports council also organizes a “Sports General Championship”, an intra-college sports fest every academic year which is the most sought after college activity in the institute. It has also maintained various club activities in the hostels and has organized adventure tours for students. As a part of in-house sports and recreational activities there are different clubs under the sports council ranging from carom, chess, skating to yoga and the adventure club.

Academic and Research Affairs (A.R.A.)

The Academic and Research Affairs Council (A.R.A) is the recent augmentation to the Student Council for Holistic Development (SCHoD) and is established with a prime motive of putting forth students’ views on Academics & Research. The Council will be the academic student body of IIT Jodhpur and will act as an intermediary between students and the institute. This council will represent the student body as an intricate part of IIT Jodhpur thereby helping students reach their full academic potential. The role of the council will further extend to promote and advance research within the institute

Functions and Responsibilities

1.To effectively represent the views of the students’ community on academic and research matters.
2.To help individual students tackle their specific academic problems and take up their problems to respective authorities.
3.To coordinate with the functioning of all centralized facilities of academic domain such as Computer Centre, Library and Reading Room amongst others.
4.To recommend revision of academic curriculum, reform policies and initiate academic and research programs.

Mission

1.To serve as a platform for students to undertake research projects under professors and to create an environment of cognizance in the student community pertaining to the reallife problems and help them to procure their research needs.
2.To strengthen Student-Faculty relationship and take them beyond the brick walls to explore the intricacies in research and come up with novel solutions.
3.To organize various technical meets and seminars to expose students to the recent discoveries and technological advancements and the innumerable opportunities that they can pursue.

Council Structure

1.General Executive, A.R.A –
The General Executive is an elected member from the Student Body, elected through General Elections conducted by Election Commission.
2.Department Representatives -
The Council has Representatives from every department in the institute. 3.Faculty Advisor -
Faculty In charge of the council.
