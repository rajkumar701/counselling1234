  • question_answerQ.1 When to reach IIT Jodhpur?

    Since the orientation programme is beginning on 20th July, students are supposed to reach the residential campus latest by 5 pm of 19th July

  • question_answerQ.2 How to reach IIT Jodhpur?

    Buses will be available at regular intervals by the Institute at railway station and Airport, student volunteers will be there to accompany you. Also bus schedule will be provided to you for convenience.

  • question_answerQ.3 When will the rooms be allotted?

    Rooms will be allotted on 19th July immediately after you arrive the residential campus

  • question_answerQ.4 Can my parents stay with me in the room or in the campus?

    Parents and Guardians are Not Allowed to stay on campus at night. They will be provided a list of hotels where they can make a booking.

  • question_answerQ.5 Will I be alloted room even before 19th July, 2014?

    You will not be provided any accommodation before 19th of July.

  • question_answerQ.6 How are the rooms allotted?

    The room allocation for students is completely random and the institute does not hold or entertain any preferences or requests in this regard. Basically in every room there is one student from every branch as we try incorporate the feature of interdisciplinary almost everywhere. A request to change the room can be made only on medical grounds. No other reasons will be entertained.

  • question_answerQ.7 How will be the weather?

    Jodhpur is known as the Sun City, for the bright and sunny weather it enjoys throughout the year. It experiences dry and hot, a typical desert weather. The average annual rainfall is approximately 32 cm. In summer, the temperatures range from a maximum of around 42 C to a minimum of around 37 C. In winters, the maximum and minimum temperatures are around 27.5 C and 15.5 C respectively.

  • question_answerQ.8 Are we allowed to bring laptops?

    Laptops are not needed in the 1st year. So we strongly suggest you not to bring laptops during the first year.

  • question_answerQ.9 Should we carry buckets, mugs, mattress and pillows?

    Buckets, mugs, pillow and mattresses will be made AVAILABLE at the residential complex itself at reasonable prizes, so you don't need to carry them. However you are advised to get bed linen, towels, alarm-clock, toiletries and sanitary items and other things that you might immediately require after you get here

  • question_answerQ.10 The Institute and the Residential Campus are separated by a distance of 14 kms. How do we do daily traveling?

    The Institute has buses plying between institute and hostels on regular basis. Both students and parents can avail this facility.

  • question_answerQ.11 What is the orientation program? Is it compulsory?

    The Orientation Program is designed to introduce the students to various facets of the IITJ life. The students will be addressed by the Director,the ScHoD President, the General Executives and various Guest Speakers. There is an interactive session between Director and Parents. For the students a special industrial tour is also arranged. All these events are planned to help student know about the institute. Thus it is advised not to miss the orientation program.

  • question_answerQ.12 Is there any dress code or uniform?
